Who we are

Built for BC. Grown from the ground up.

The Boaz Club is a BC-based farming venture on a simple mission: make it possible for anyone to grow their own food, anywhere, all year round.

The Fraser Valley is one of the most fertile agricultural regions in Canada. Yet most of it sits idle in winter, most residents buy their vegetables from thousands of kilometres away, and most land owners earn nothing from their agricultural properties year-round.

The Boaz Club changes that. By bringing greenhouse infrastructure, technology, and a community of growers together under one roof, we turn underused land into living, producing, income-generating spaces, open to everyone.

The name Boaz comes from the Biblical landowner who opened his fields to his community, ensuring everyone had access to what the land could provide. That spirit is at the heart of everything we do.

🌿

Organic by default

No pesticides, no synthetic inputs. Every plot uses certified organic soil and natural growing practices.

🏠

Community at the core

Shared orchards, Pod Stores, and workshops make The Boaz Club a neighbourhood resource, not just a rental service.

🏭

Technology that disappears

Smart irrigation, LED lighting, and climate control work in the background so growers focus on growing.
