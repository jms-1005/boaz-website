Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The Boaz Club ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect through our website (boaz.club) and related sign-up forms, how we use it, and the choices you have. We operate in British Columbia, Canada, and handle personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and B.C.'s Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you join our waitlist, register interest as a landowner, enquire as a restaurant, or contact us — on our website or through forms hosted on social platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — we may collect your name, email address, and details you choose to provide (for example, your preferred plot size, land size, or business name).

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we use analytics to understand how our site is used. Through Google Analytics and similar tools we may collect non-identifying technical data such as your approximate location, device and browser type, pages visited, and referring links, typically via cookies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and add you to our waitlist or interest list;
  • To contact you with updates about The Boaz Club, including launch news and early-member offers;
  • To understand and improve our website and service; and
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations.

We rely on your consent to send you marketing or waitlist communications, and you can withdraw that consent at any time (see "Your choices and rights" below).

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate, and only as needed:

  • HubSpot — to manage our forms, waitlist, and contact records (customer relationship management);
  • Google Analytics — to measure website usage;
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — where you submit a form through their platforms.

These providers may store and process data outside British Columbia, including in the United States. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies for analytics. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; some site features may not function as intended if you do.

Data retention

We keep your personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, or as required by law. If you ask us to remove you, we will delete your information from our active records.

Your choices and rights

You may unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link in any message. Subject to applicable law, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to ask us to correct it, and to ask us to delete it or stop contacting you. To make a request, email us at privacy@boaz.club.

Children's privacy

Our website and sign-up forms are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent version, and material changes will be posted on this page.

Contact us

Questions or requests about this policy or your personal information can be sent to:
The Boaz Club · privacy@boaz.club · British Columbia, Canada
